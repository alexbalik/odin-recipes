Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is a popular Italian dish passed down from immigrants who came to the US in the 1950's

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Bread the chicken: Bread the chicken breasts and lightly brown them in a pan
  2. Add marinara sauce: Place them into a casserole dish with marinara sauce and top with cheese
  3. Top with cheese and bake: Bake until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is golden brown and bubbly
